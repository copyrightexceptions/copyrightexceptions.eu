date: 2021-06-21

exceptions:
- dsm6
jurisdictions:
- DE
score: 3
description: "This exception allows for the reproduction for the purpose of preservation, by publicly accessible libraries (section 60e), as well as archives, institutions in the field of cinematic and audio heritage, publicly accessible museums and educational establishments (section 60f) of works from their holdings or exhibitions." 
beneficiaries:
- publicly accessible libraries 
- archives, institutions in the field of cinematic and audio heritage, publicly accessible museums and educational establishments 
purposes: 
- preservation
usage:
- reproduction
subjectmatter:
- works
- performances
- phonograms
- broadcasts
- film fixations
- press publications
compensation:
- no compensation required for reproductions for the purpose of preservation in accordance with section 60e (1) and section 60f (1)
attribution: 
- the source, including the name of the author, must in all cases be acknowledged unless this is not possible
otherConditions: 
- works used must be from beneficiaries' holdings or exhibitions
remarks: "The implementation of art.6 of the CDSM Directive builds on the existing implementation of art.5(2)(c) of the InfoSoc Directive by broadening the pre-existing reproduction exception to also cover publicly accessible institutions operating for commercial purposes, insofar as preservation is concerned.<br /><br />The exception applies accordingly to performances (§83 UrhG), phonograms (§85(4) UrhG), broadcasts (§87(4) UrhG), press publications (§87i UrhG) and film fixations (§94 UrhG)."
