remarks: "According to art. 44b, para 1, 'text and data mining' is the automated analysis of individual or multiple digital or digitized works in order to extract information, particularly about patterns, trends and correlations.<br /><br />Use is only permissible if the rightsholder has not reserved it. A reservation of use for works accessible online is only effective if it is made in machine-readable form. Under a general provision in §60h, authors are entitled to equitable remuneration. Claims to equitable remuneration may only be asserted through a collecting society. This added condition warrants a lower score for the exception.<br /><br />The exception applies accordingly to performances (§83), phonograms (§85(4)), broadcasts (§87(4)), press publications (§87i) and film fixations (§94 UrhG), as well as 'sui generis' databases (§87c(1)(5)). Under §95b, the exception is safeguarded against circumvention by TPMs."
