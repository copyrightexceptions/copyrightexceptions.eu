description: ""This exception allows for reproductions, by any user, for text and data mining of legally accessible works and other subject matter." 
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes: 
- any purpose
usage:
- reproduction
subjectmatter:
- works
- performances
- phonograms
- broadcasts
- film fixations
- press publications
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- no attribution required
otherConditions: 
- works must be legally accessible (§ 44b(2) UrhG)
- use must have not been reserved by the rightsholder
- reproductions must be deleted when they are no longer required for text and data mining (§ 44b(2) UrhG)
remarks: "According to art. 44b, para 1, 'text and data mining' is the automated analysis of individual or multiple digital or digitized works in order to extract information, particularly about patterns, trends and correlations.<br /><br />Use is only permissible if the rightsholder has not reserved it. A reservation of use for works accessible online is only effective if it is made in machine-readable form.<br /><br />The exception applies accordingly to performances (§83), phonograms (§85(4)), broadcasts (§87(4)), press publications (§87i) and film fixations (§94 UrhG)."
