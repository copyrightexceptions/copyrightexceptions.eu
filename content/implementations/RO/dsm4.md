description: "This exception allows for the reproductions and extractions from legally accessible works and other protected subject matter for the purpose of text and data extraction.
The exception applies provided that the use of works and other protected subject matter has not been expressly reserved by the rightholders in an appropriate manner, such as machine-readable means in the case of content made public online."

- any user

- film fixations
- press publications
- databases

- no compensation required

- no attribution required
otherConditions: 
- lawful access
- use of works and other protected subject matter must not have been expressly reserved by the rightholders in an appropriate manner, such as machine-readable means in the case of content made public online
- "storage and reuse: reproductions and extractions may be kept for as long as is necessary for the extraction of the text and data"
remarks: "The application of the exception does not prejudice the application of art. 36^1. Safeguards against TPMs appear not to extend to it (art. 36^5(3)).<br /><br/>According to art. 120 of the Romanian Law No 8/1996 on copyright and related rights, the provisions regarding the limits of copyright also apply accordingly to related rights. It's unclear if the exception applies to broadcasts, as it is not referenced in the provision of art. 134. Art. 94^1 makes all exceptions applicable to press publications. As per art. 36^1(2), this particular exception also applies to sui generis database rights."
