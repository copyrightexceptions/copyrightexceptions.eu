title: "Section 7(2)(g), 1st sentence, of the Copyright Law"

exceptions:
- info53c-1
jurisdictions:
- CY
score: 3
description: "This exception allows for the reproduction by the press, presentation to the public or the making available of published articles on economic, political or religious current affairs or of works or other objects of the same type broadcast by radio or television, when such use is not expressly prohibited and provided that the source is indicated, including the name of the author." 
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes: 
- not specified (informatory in its nature)
usage:
- reproduction (by the press)
- presentation to the public
- making available 
subjectmatter:
- published articles (on economic, political or religious current affairs)
- works or other objects of the same type 
- phonograms (on economic, political or religious current affairs)
- film fixations (on economic, political or religious current affairs)
- broadcasts (on economic, political or religious current affairs)
- press publications (on economic, political or religious current affairs)
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- the source, including the name of the author, msut be indicated
otherConditions: 
- articles used must be published
- works used must be on economic, political or religious current affairs
- use must not be expressly prohibited 
remarks: "In terms of subject matter, Cyprus' exceptions apply to 'intellectual property'. According to Section 3(1)(a) of the Law, the material scope of protection of 'intellectual property' includes works, incl. computer programmes, but also databases, phonograms, broadcasts and publications of previously unpublished works. Furthermore, Section 3(1)(c)(iii) of the Law states that the exceptions and limitations recognized in art. 3 and in art. 7 vis-a-vis 'intellectual property' also apply to related rights, in the absence of a contrary provision. According to Section 3(1)(b) the objects of related rights are press publications and performances."
