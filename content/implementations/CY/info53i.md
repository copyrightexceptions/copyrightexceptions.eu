title: "Section 7(2)(d) of the Copyright Law"
date: 1976
draft: false
weight: 59
exceptions:
- info53i
jurisdictions:
- CY
score: 2
description: "This exception allows for the the incidental inclusion of an artistic work in a film or broadcast."
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes: 
- any purpose (but use has to be incidental)
usage:
- inclusion in a film or broadcast
subjectmatter:
- artistic work
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- no attribution required
otherConditions: 
- use must be incidental
remarks: "Similar to the Cypriot Freedom of Panorama exception, the incidental inclusion one has a limited scope in terms of types of usage. It only covers the inclusion in a film or broadcast. Although there are no further conditions for the permitted use, the restricted types of permitted use warrants a lower score.<br /><br/>In terms of subject matter, this specific exception only covers artistic works. However, Cyprus' exceptions as a whole apply to 'intellectual property'. According to Section 3(1)(a) of the Law, the material scope of protection of 'intellectual property' includes works, incl. computer programmes, but also databases, phonograms, broadcasts and publications of previously unpublished works. Furthermore, Section 3(1)(c)(iii) of the Law states that the exceptions and limitations recognized in art. 3 and in art. 7 vis-a-vis 'intellectual property' also apply to related rights, in the absence of a contrary provision. According to Section 3(1)(b) the objects of related rights are press publications and performances."
