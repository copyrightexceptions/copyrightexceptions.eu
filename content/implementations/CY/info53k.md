remarks: "Cyprus implemented art. 5(3)(k) of the InfoSoc Directive in 2022, in the context of transposing art. 17 of the CDSM Directive. Section 7(2)(k), however, is not limited to platform uses and has a broad scope, closely aligned with the InfoSoc clause. A potential issue arises from the translation of the term 'pastiche' as 'imitation'.<br /><br/>In terms of subject matter, Cyprus' exceptions apply to 'intellectual property'. According to Section 3(1)(a) of the Law, the material scope of protection of 'intellectual property' includes works, incl. computer programmes, but also databases, phonograms, broadcasts and publications of previously unpublished works. Furthermore, Section 3(1)(c)(iii) of the Law states that the exceptions and limitations recognized in art. 3 and in art. 7 vis-a-vis 'intellectual property' also apply to related rights, in the absence of a contrary provision. According to Section 3(1)(b) the objects of related rights are press publications and performances."
