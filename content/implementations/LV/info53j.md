description: "This exception allows for the use visual and art works (drawings, paintings, sculptures, graphic works and other works of art; works of applied art, decorations and scenography; design works; photographic works and works executed in a manner similar to photography) or fragments thereof for the purpose of advertising a public exhibition or public sale of works of art, to the extent necessary for the promotion of the relevant event, without using them for any other commercial purpose."
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes:
- advertising a public exhibition or public sale of works of art
- non-commercial
usage:
- any use
subjectmatter:
- drawings, paintings, sculptures, graphic works and other works of art
- works of applied art, decorations and scenography
- design works
- photographic works and works executed in a manner similar to photography
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- no attribution required
otherConditions: 
- works used must be of specific nature (mostly visual artworks)
- use mustne non-commercial
- use is limited to the extent necessary for the promotion of the relevant event
- use is subject to the three-step test (art.18(2))
remarks: "This exception was only introducced in 2023. It does not extend to any neighbouring rights as per Section 54, para 3.2 of the CA."
