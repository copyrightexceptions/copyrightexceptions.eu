- LV

description: "This exception allows cultural heritage institutions to make available to the public, for non-commercial purposes, works or objects of related rights permanently in their collection that are not available for commercial circulation, by wire or in another way, in such a way that they can be accessed at an individually chosen place and at an individually chosen time on a non-commercial website, unless (i) the management of the economic rights of the relevant copyright or related rights is not carried out by any representative collective management organisation, and (ii) copyright or related rights holders have not requested cease of the use." 
beneficiaries:
- cultural heritage institutions 
purposes: 
- non-commercial
usage:
- making available to the public (on a non-commercial website)
subjectmatter:
- works
- performances
- phonograms
- film fixations
- broadcasts
- databases
- press publications
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- the name of the author or any other identifiable copyright or related rights holder must be indicated
otherConditions: 
- works used must be permanently in beneficiaries' collections
- the management of the economic rights of the relevant copyright or related rights must not be carried out by any collective management organisation that has received a permit from the Ministry of Culture for the management of the specific rights and type of works or related rights objects in accordance with the Law on Collective Management of Copyright
- copyright or related rights holders must have not requested that the cultural heritage institution cease the use of their works or related rights in whole or in relation to individual works or related rights
- the place of use is deemed to be a Member State of the European Union or a country of the European Economic Area in which the relevant cultural heritage institution is established
