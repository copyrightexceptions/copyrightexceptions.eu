title: "Section 42h, para 6 of the of the Federal Law on Copyright in Literary and Artistic Works and Related Rights"
date: 2021-06-07

exceptions:
- dsm3
jurisdictions:
- AT
score: 3
description: "This exception allows users to reproduce works in order to automatically evaluate texts and data in digital form for scientific or artistic research and to obtain information, including on patterns, trends, and correlations, provided they have lawful access to the work and reproduction is not expressly prohibited."
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes:
- any purpose (in order to automatically analyze texts and data in digital form and obtain information)
usage:
- reproduction
- extraction
subjectmatter:
- works
- photographs
- phonograms
- broadcasts
- press publications
- databases
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution:
- no attribution required
otherConditions:
- lawful access
- "storage and reuse: beneficiaries have the right to store and retain TDM results as long as necessary for the purposes of data analysis and information gathering"
- reproduction must not have been expressly prohibited
remarks: "The excetion is not protected against contractual override, but is safegarded against circumvention by ways of TPMs. There is an obligation for holders to 'immediately' provide access to works, insofar as beneficiaries have lawful access to the subject matter, so they can make use of the exception to the required extent (§90c(6)).<br /><br />The exception applies to photographs and cinematographic products (§74(7)), performances (§71(6)), phonograms (§76(4)), broadcasts (§76a(3)), sui generis database rights (§76d(5)), press publications (§76f(5)).<br /><br />Opt-out must be appropriately indicated by a reservation of rights, for example, using machine-readable means in the case of works made publicly accessible via the internet. Still, in the absence of a formal opt-out, there is an obligation for rightsholders to 'immediately' provide access to works, insofar as beneficiaries have lawful access to the subject matter, so they can make use of the exception to the required extent."
