description: "Austria provides for a limited exception for digital use for the purpose of illustration for teaching in §42g. In addition, under §42(6), schools, universities, and other educational institutions may, for the purposes of instruction and teaching, produce and distribute copies in the number required for a specific class or course (reproduction for their own school use), to the extent justified by such use. This also applies to sheet music. Regarding digital materials, this use is only permitted for non-commercial purposes. The right to reproduce for their own school use does not apply to works that, by their nature and designation, are intended for school or teaching use." 
beneficiaries:
- any user (under the responsibility of the educational institution on its premises or at other locations or in a secure electronic environment)
purposes: 
- illustrating teaching or instruction
- support, enrich or supplement teaching or instruction
- own school use (reproduction and distribution only)
usage:
- reproduction
- distribution
- broadcasting
- making available to the public
subjectmatter:
- works
- photographs
- phonograms
- broadcasts
- press publications
compensation:
- the author is entitled to an appropriate remuneration, that can only be asserted by collecting societies (§42g) 
- storage media remuneration as per §42b (§42(6))
attribution: 
- no explicit attribution requirement
otherConditions: 
- use must be justified for the pursuit of non-commercial purposes
- use under §42g must take place in a secure electronic environment, to which only the pupils, students and teaching staff of the educational institution have access
- use under §42g of 'works intended for school or educational use according to their nature and designation', as well as of 'cinematic works whose initial screening took place either domestically or in German or in a language of an ethnic group recognized in Austria no more than two years prior' may not exceed minor excerpts of the work, up to 10%
- individual works of fine art and depictions of the type, or other works of minor scope and out-of-print works may be used in their entirety
- the exception under §42g does not apply if permissions for use can be obtained under reasonable conditions
- the rightsholder can 'opt out' of the use under §42g, by creating general terms and conditions for the use of the works accessible via the internet and ensure that they can respond promptly to requests for usage rights
- use is considered to take place in the country in which the educational institution is located
remarks: "The provision explicitly covers use of public performance (§18(3)) , as well as databases (§40g). In addition, the exception applies to photographs and cinematographic products (§74(7)), performances (§71(6)), phonograms (§76(4)), broadcasts (§76a(3)), sui generis database rights (§76d(5)) and press publications (§76f(5))."
