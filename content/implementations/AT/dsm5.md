- dsm5

- AT
score: 1
description: "This exception allows schools, universities and other educational institutions to reproduce, distribute, broadcast and make available to the public works and other sibject-matter, for the purpose of illustrating teaching or instruction, in particular to support, enrich or supplement it. The use must take place under the responsibility of the educational institution on its premises or at other locations or in a secure electronic environment, to which only the pupils, students and teaching staff of the educational institution have access, and insofar as this is justified for the pursuit of non-commercial purposes." 
beneficiaries:
- any user (under the responsibility of the educational institution on its premises or at other locations or in a secure electronic environment)
purposes: 
- illustrating teaching or instruction
- support, enrich or supplement teaching or instruction
usage:
- reproduction
- distribution
- broadcasting
- making available to the public
subjectmatter:
- works
- photographs
- databases
- performances
- phonograms
- broadcasts
- press publications
compensation:
- the author is entitled to an appropriate remuneration, that can only be asserted by collecting societies
attribution: 
- no attribution requirements mentioned
otherConditions: 
- use must be justified for the pursuit of non-commercial purposes
- use in a secure electronic environment, to which only the pupils, students and teaching staff of the educational institution have access
- use of 'works intended for school or educational use according to their nature and designation', as well as of 'cinematic works whose initial screening took place either domestically or in German or in a language of an ethnic group recognized in Austria no more than two years prior' may not exceed minor excerpts of the work, up to 10%
- individual works of fine art and depictions of the type, or other works of minor scope and out-of-print works may be used in their entirety
- the exception does not apply if permissions for use can be obtained under reasonable conditions
- the rightsholder can 'opt out' of the use, by creating general terms and conditions for the use of the works accessible via the internet and ensure that they can respond promptly to requests for usage rights
- use is considered to take place in the country in which the educational institution is located
remarks: "The provision explicitly covers use of public performance (§18(3)) , as well as databases (§40g). In addition, the exception applies to photographs and cinematographic products (§74(7)), performances (§71(6)), phonograms (§76(4)), broadcasts (§76a(3)), sui generis database rights (§76d(5)) and press publications (§76f(5))."
