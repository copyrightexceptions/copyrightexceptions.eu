description: "This exception allows for the digital use of works, for the exclusive purpose of illustration in the context of teaching, to the extent justified by the non-commercial purpose pursued, provided that this usetakes place under the responsibility of an educational institution, on its premises or elsewhere, or through a secure electronic environment accessible only to the pupils, students, and teaching staff of that institution." 
beneficiaries:
- any user (under the responsibility of an educational institution, on its premises or elsewhere, or through a secure environment) 
purposes: 
- illustration in the context of teaching
- non-commercial
usage:
- any use
subjectmatter:
- works (incl. computer programmes and databases)
- performances
- phonograms
- film fixations
- broadcasts
- press publications
- databases
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- use must be accompanied by an indication of the source, including the name of the author, unless this proves impossible
otherConditions: 
- use must take place under the responsibility of an educational institution, on its premises or elsewhere or through a secure electronic environment accessible only to the pupils, students, and teaching staff of that institution
- use carried out using secure electronic environments is deemed to take place only in the Member State of the European Union in which the educational establishment is located
remarks: "In 2022, the national legislator added a general rule in a new paragraph 4 of art.10, according to which all exceptions are imperative (non-overriddable by contract).<br /><br/>The scope of the exception is extended to neighbouring rights under art. 46 (1), 9° and art. 46 (2) - for performers, phonogram and film producers; art. 55 refers to art. 46 for broadcasters and art.56bis(3) - for ress publishers. The exception also covers the sui generis database right (art. 68(2)). All exceptions apply mutatis mutandis to the distribution right."
