description: "This exception allows for the reproduction of works or other protected materials, provided that they are legally accessible, for the purpose of texts and data mining, provided that such use has not been expressly reserved by the rightsholder in an appropriate manner, in particular through machine readable means in the case of content made available to the public online." 
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes: 
- texts and data mining
usage:
- reproduction
subjectmatter:
- works
- performances
- phonograms
- film fixations
- broadcasts
- press publications
- databases
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- no attribution specifically required
otherConditions: 
- lawful access
- use must not have been reserved by the rightsholder in an appropriate manner (through machine readable means in the case of content made available to the public online) 
remarks: "The exception applies mutatis mutandis to neighbouring rights under art. 189(3) of the Copyright code. Coverage of databases is regulated in a different law on databases (Decreto-Lei 122*2000).<br /><br/>Indication of source is mandatory in Portugal for all exceptions. Under art. 76º n.º1 (a), the free use must be accompanied by the indication, whenever possible, of the name of the author and editor, the title of the work and other circumstances that identify them.<br /><br/>Safeguards against circumvention by way of TPMs are set in arts 221(1) and 222 of the Law. However, there are no identified safeguards against contractual override and TPMs concerning sui generis databases."
