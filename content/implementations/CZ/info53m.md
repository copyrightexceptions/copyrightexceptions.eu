description: "This exception allows for (i) the rental, lending or exhibition of originals or the reproduction of works of applied art expressed in a usable form or an architectural work expressed in a building, as well as (ii) the use of architectural works expressed by a building, drawing or plan for the purposes of maintenance work or changes to the completed building to the extent strictly necessary and while preserving the value of the architectural work. In the second case, the user - if this is justified by the significance of the architectural work and if it can be fairly demanded of it - is obliged to inform the author in advance of his intention and, upon request, provide the latter with documentation of the building, including images, describing the state before the changes were made." 
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes: 
- any purpose (rental, lending, exhibition and reproduction of architectural works)
- maintenance or changes to the completed building 
usage:
- rental, lending, exhibition and reproduction (1st case)
- any use (2nd case)
subjectmatter:
- architectural works
compensation:
- no compensation required 
attribution: 
- no attribution required
otherConditions: 
- architectural works used must be expressed in a building, drawing or plan
- use must be limited to the extent strictly necessary, while preserving the value of the architectural work
- if this is justified by the significance of the architectural work and if it can be fairly demanded of it, the user is obliged to inform the author in advance of his intention and, upon request, provide the latter with documentation of the building, including images, describing the state before the changes were made
remarks: "Pursuant to §29(2), works used must be disclosed.<br /><br />§29(3) contains a general provision regarding the imperative nature of exceptions. It states that 'any legal act that excludes or limits copyright exceptions or limitations shall be disregarded, unless the law provides otherwise'.<br /><br />The exception extends to performers' (§ 74), phonogram producers' (§ 78), film producers' (§ 82), broadcasters' (§ 86) and press publishers' rights (§87b(7))."
