description: "This exception allows users of information society services to make works available for caricature, parody or pastiche purposes on such a service."

- users of information society services

otherConditions:
- use is allowed only on information society services
remarks: "The exception was only introduced in 2023 through the implementation of art. 17 of the CDSM Directive and is limited to making available on information society services by their users. It is not formulated as a self-standing exception in Chapter 2 of the Law on copyright limitations, but rather as a part of the platform liability regime in Chapter 6b, entitled 'Special provisions on certain online content sharing services'. Furthermore, the exception only covers works. it does not extend to any neighbouring rights.<br /><br/>Art. 11(2) requires that when a work is used publicly under an exception, the source must be, as a general rule, stated to the extent and in the manner required by proper usage. It also requires that the work must not be altered more than necessary for the intended use."
