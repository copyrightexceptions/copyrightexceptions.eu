title: "Article 22<sup>1</sup> of the Law No VIII-1185 on Copyright and Related Rights"
date: 2022-03-24
draft: false
weight: 10
exceptions:
- dsm3
jurisdictions:
- LT
score: 3
description: "This exception allows scientific research organisations and cultural heritage institutions to reproduce works for non-commercial purposes in order to extract texts and data from works to which they have lawful access for the purposes of scientific research." 
beneficiaries:
- scientific research organisations
- cultural heritage institutions
purposes: 
- non-commercial
- scientific research
subjectmatter:
- works
- performances
- phonograms
- film fixations 
- broadcasts
- electronic press publications
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- the source and the author's name must be acknowledged where possible
otherConditions: 
- lawful access
- "storage and re: copies of works must be stored ensuring restrictions on the use and/or distribution of texts and data and may be retained for the purposes of scientific research, including in order to verify the results of scientific research"
- "safeguards: copyright holders are permitted to apply measures to ensure the security and integrity of computer networks and databases in which works are stored"
- contractual terms that do not allow the application of the exception are null and void
remarks: "The exception also covers neighbouring rights under art 58(4), p.15 of the Law, according to which 'the provisions of Articles 22<sup>1</sup>, 22<sup>2</sup> and 23 of this Law shall apply mutatis mutandis to restrictions on related economic rights.'."
