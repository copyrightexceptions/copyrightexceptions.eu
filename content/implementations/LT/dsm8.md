description: "This exception allows cultural heritage institutions to use works or other objects in their collections that are no longer in commerce for non-commercial purposes, to reproduce, distribute, communicate to the public or make publicly available via computer networks (on the Internet). Works must be used within beneficiaries' fields of activity, in the course of their normal activities related to the purposes of public interest in the fields of cultural dissemination, heritage protection, education, science and public information.
The exception applies when cultural heritage institutions do not have the conditions to obtain a license from a collective management organization, "

- making available to the public

- works

- broadcasts

compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- the names of the authors and/or other rightholders and the sources used must be indicated, if possible
otherConditions: 
- works used must be in beneficiaries' collections
- beneficiaries must be established in the territory of the Republic of Lithuania
- use must occur in the course of beneficiaries' normal activities related to the purposes of public interest in the fields of cultural dissemination, heritage protection, education, science and public information
- the exception only applies where cultural heritage institutions are unable to obtain the licence provided for in Article 65ยน of the Law from a collective management organisation  (because there is no collective management organisation meeting the requirements set out in Article 65ยน(3) of this Law, or because such organisation does not carry out collective management of certain types of works or other subject matter)
remarks: "Pursuant to art. 97(1) the mechanism covers works, related rights subject matter, or sui generis rights subject matter that are out of commerce. According to art.97(3), 'a work or other object shall be deemed to be permanently in the collections or holdings of a cultural heritage institution when it is owned by the cultural heritage institution or transferred by right of trust'."
