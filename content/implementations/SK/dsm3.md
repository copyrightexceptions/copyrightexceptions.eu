remarks: "Although the Slovak implementation of the TDM exception generally follows Article 3 of the CDSM Directive, it appears to cover only cultural heritage institutions and schools as beneficiaries. In other words, research organisations (at least other than Universities) seem not to be included in its scope. Despite the very exception-friendly approach of the Slovak legislator, this single but significant omission justifies a low score for the exception.<br /><br/>With the implementation of the CDSM Directive, Slovakia introduced a general prohibition of contractual override of exceptions in a new § 35(4). A for technical protection measures, pursuant to § 60(4), the prohibition on the circumvention of TPMs does not apply to the use of a work under § 51b, to the extent necessary to exercise the relevant exception or limitation.<br /><br/>The exception applies to performers' (§ 103), phonogram producers' (§113), audiovisual producers' (§121) and broadcasters' (§127.1), press publishers' (§129e) and sui generis database rights (§138.4.e)."
